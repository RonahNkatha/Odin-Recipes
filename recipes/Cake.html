Cake Baking

Description

Cake is one of the easiest to go to dessert around the world

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk, full fat
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs with sugar for a whole 7 minutes until thick and glossy, it changes from yellow to white, and tripled in volume (just over double depth in the bowl).
  2. Whisk together the flour, baking powder and salt. Then add into egg mixture in 3 lots, mixing for just 5 seconds on Speed 1 in between. This should make the flour just incorporated
  3. use a microwave or stove to heat milk with the butter until it melts.
  4. Mix some butter into hot milk
  5. Slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs
  6. Pour into cake pans
  7. Bake for 30 minutes
  8. Cool upside down
  9. Decorate
